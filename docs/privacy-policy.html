WorthIt Android app

Privacy Policy

WorthIt helps you understand whether subscriptions are worth their cost by comparing what you pay with how much you use them. This policy explains what data is stored on your device and what limited analytics and crash data may be collected to improve the app.

No accounts WorthIt does not currently require or offer user accounts.
Local app data Subscription and usage details you enter are stored locally on your device.
No ads or sales WorthIt does not show ads and does not sell your personal data.

Who We Are

WorthIt is provided by Cameron Dewell. This Privacy Policy applies to the WorthIt Android app and describes how information is handled when you use the app.

Effective date:12 June 2026

What WorthIt Does

WorthIt lets you manually track subscriptions, subscription costs, billing cycles, and usage data so you can estimate the cost per hour or other value metrics for each subscription. WorthIt is designed as a personal tracking tool.

Information You Enter in the App

You may choose to enter information such as subscription names, costs, billing cycles, categories, renewal details, and usage records. This information is stored locally on your device. WorthIt does not currently provide user accounts, cloud sync, or server-side storage for this information.

Because this information is stored locally, deleting the app or clearing the app's data may delete your WorthIt data from your device. Your device operating system, app store, or backup settings may handle device data separately from WorthIt.

Firebase Analytics

WorthIt uses Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and improve the app. This may include information such as app opens, feature interactions, app version, device type, operating system version, general region, language, and other diagnostic or usage metrics made available by Firebase Analytics.

We do not use Firebase Analytics to intentionally collect the subscription names, costs, billing cycles, or usage records that you manually enter into WorthIt.

Firebase Crashlytics

WorthIt uses Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics. If the app crashes or has a serious error, Crashlytics may collect information such as crash logs, stack traces, app version, device model, operating system version, crash time, installation identifiers, and related diagnostic data.

Crashlytics helps identify and fix bugs. We do not intentionally include your manually entered subscription details in crash reports.

How We Share Information

WorthIt does not sell your personal data. WorthIt does not use advertising networks and does not share your data for targeted advertising.

Analytics and crash/diagnostic data may be processed by Google Firebase as our service provider. Google Firebase processes this data to provide analytics, crash reporting, and related infrastructure services. You can learn more from the Google Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

We may also disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or an enforceable governmental request.

No Accounts, Ads, Payments, or Sale of Data

  • WorthIt does not currently use user accounts.
  • WorthIt does not currently provide cloud sync.
  • WorthIt does not show third-party ads.
  • WorthIt does not process payments in the app.
  • WorthIt does not sell your personal data.

Children's Privacy

WorthIt is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You choose what subscription and usage information to enter into WorthIt.
  • You can edit or delete your locally stored subscription and usage data in the app.
  • You can delete locally stored WorthIt data by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
  • Your device settings may provide controls for app diagnostics, privacy, backups, or advertising identifiers.
  • You can contact us with privacy questions or requests using the contact details below.
